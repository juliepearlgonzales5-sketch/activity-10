About Ozamis City

Discover the heart of Misamis Occidental

The City of Good Life

Ozamis City, officially the City of Ozamiz, is a 3rd class component city in the province of Misamis Occidental, Philippines. It serves as the capital of the province and is the center of commerce, education, and government in the region.

Named after José Ozámiz, a revolutionary leader and delegate to the Malolos Congress, the city has a rich history dating back to Spanish colonial times. The famous Fort Santiago (Cotta Fort) stands as a testament to its historical significance.

With a population of over 140,000, Ozamis City offers a perfect blend of urban development and natural attractions, making it an ideal destination for both tourists and investors.

Culture & Heritage

Rich traditions and vibrant community life

Archdiocese of Ozamis

The spiritual heart of the city, the Archdiocese serves the Catholic community with historic churches and vibrant religious celebrations.

Local Cuisine & Food Parks

Experience authentic Ozameño cuisine at local food parks featuring fresh seafood, native delicacies, and fusion dishes.

Heritage Tours

Explore the rich Spanish colonial heritage through guided tours of historic sites, ancestral houses, and cultural landmarks.
